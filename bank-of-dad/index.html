Bank of Dad

We have an issue in our household where we'll be at a store & one of the kids will tell me “I have $3 at home, can I buy this?” And reader, it turns out they did not have $3 at home.

So, now they can deposit money at home with me, and I make note of it in the site we vibe coded last night, Bank of Dad.

Storage is handled locally using LocalStorage.

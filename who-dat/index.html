Who Dat

A web app that turns 24 photos into a custom game of "Who Dat?" — a personalized take on Guess Who.

Drag your photos onto the page, and OpenAI's DALL-E 3 transforms each one into a colorful cartoon portrait playing card. The result is a printable PDF with cards, rules, and assembly instructions — ready to cut out and slot into a standard Guess Who board.

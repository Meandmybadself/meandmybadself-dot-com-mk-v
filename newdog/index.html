New Dog

A successor to Old Dog, whose battery eventually ballooned inside the device and popped the screen off.

This version runs on a Raspberry Pi Zero driving a cheap 15.6" 1080p portable LED monitor. We simplified the stack by rebuilding the display with web components and added birthday countdowns for each family member along with sunrise/sunset times and air quality index.

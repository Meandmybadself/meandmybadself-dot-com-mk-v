Regency Beauty

Select Stylist

Select Stylist was built to test a hypothesis:

Could we use a variety of factors to find customers a better hair styling experience?

We surveyed stylists on their self-perceived hair styling experiences & compared that against customers' ideal experience.

The site was designed & developed as a mobile-first experience & ran from 2015-2019.

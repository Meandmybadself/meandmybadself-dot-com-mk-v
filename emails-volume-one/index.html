Emails Volume One

My wife and I met under unusual circumstances when I accidentally sent an email to the wrong domain.

For our 20th anniversary, I downloaded all email messages between the two of us into SQLite, then used Python and Pillow to generate a PDF of our first year of emails, set in Google Sans.

I had it printed as a book and surprised her with it on October 23rd, 2025.

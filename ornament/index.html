Ornament

The kids were all home sick with the flu, so instead of watching TV we made ornaments from their sketches.

The pipeline: draw the ornament, feed the sketch to ChatGPT to clean it up and render it as a 3D object, pass that render to Meshy to generate an STL, print it, and paint it.

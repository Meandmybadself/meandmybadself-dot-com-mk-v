City of Pawnee Employee Directory

As part of a technical interview for a company that I would have loved to have worked for, I was asked to build an employee directory.

The result was a tribute to one of my favorite TV shows,
Parks and Recreation

The City of Pawnee Employee Directory includes information about all of the employees of the City of Pawnee. The information includes the employee's name, title, phone number, email address, and a photo of the employee.

The application aims for a "city government" aesthetic and was designed while developed with Tailwind CSS.

It's content is editable with a custom CMS powered by Feathers.
